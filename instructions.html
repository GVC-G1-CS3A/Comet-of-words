How To Play

Type all the words that appear on the screen. There is a yellow line at the bottom of the screen.
DO NOT LET THE WORD REACHES THE BOTTOM AS MUCH AS POSSIBLE

For every minute, rage mode will be activated. Bunch of words will fall at the same time.
MAKE SURE TO KEEP WATCH OF THE TIME

Score added and deducted will be based on the length of the word

Word Length Added Points Loss Points
Less than 5 3 1
Greater than 5, less than 9 4 2
Greater than or Equal to 9 5 3
Start the Game
